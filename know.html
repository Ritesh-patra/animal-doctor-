Dog Health Tips

Nutrition & Diet

  • • Feed high-quality dog food appropriate for age
  • • Avoid chocolate, grapes, onions, and garlic
  • • Maintain regular feeding schedule
  • • Always provide fresh water

Exercise & Activity

  • • Daily walks (30-60 minutes minimum)
  • • Mental stimulation with toys and games
  • • Regular playtime and socialization
  • • Adjust exercise based on breed and age

Health Monitoring

  • • Regular vet checkups (annually)
  • • Keep vaccinations up to date
  • • Monitor weight and appetite changes
  • • Check for lumps, bumps, or skin issues

Grooming & Hygiene

  • • Regular brushing (daily for long-haired breeds)
  • • Dental care - brush teeth regularly
  • • Nail trimming every 2-3 weeks
  • • Clean ears weekly

Cat Health Tips

Nutrition & Diet

  • • High-protein, low-carb diet
  • • Wet food helps with hydration
  • • Avoid milk, onions, and chocolate
  • • Multiple small meals throughout day

Indoor Environment

  • • Clean litter box daily
  • • Provide scratching posts
  • • Safe hiding spots and perches
  • • Interactive toys for mental stimulation

Health Care

  • • Annual vet visits and vaccinations
  • • Spay/neuter for health benefits
  • • Monitor urination habits
  • • Watch for hairball issues

Grooming

  • • Regular brushing prevents matting
  • • Dental care is crucial
  • • Trim nails every 2 weeks
  • • Check ears for mites or infection

Rabbit Health Tips

Diet & Nutrition

  • • Unlimited timothy hay daily
  • • High-fiber pellets (1/4 cup per 5 lbs)
  • • Fresh vegetables daily
  • • Limited fruits as treats

Housing & Environment

  • • Large cage with room to hop
  • • Safe area for daily exercise
  • • Comfortable bedding (not cedar)
  • • Protection from extreme temperatures

Health Monitoring

  • • Annual vet checkups
  • • Monitor eating and bathroom habits
  • • Watch for dental problems
  • • Check for mites and parasites

Grooming Care

  • • Regular brushing prevents hairballs
  • • Trim nails monthly
  • • Clean ears gently
  • • Never bathe unless absolutely necessary

Cow Health Tips

Nutrition & Feed

  • • Quality pasture grass and hay
  • • Balanced mineral supplements
  • • Fresh, clean water (30-50 gallons/day)
  • • Avoid moldy or spoiled feed

Disease Prevention

  • • Regular vaccinations schedule
  • • Quarantine new animals
  • • Monitor for respiratory issues
  • • Maintain clean living conditions

Health Monitoring

  • • Daily observation for illness signs
  • • Regular hoof trimming and care
  • • Monitor body condition score
  • • Check for parasites regularly

Housing & Comfort

  • • Adequate shelter from weather
  • • Proper ventilation in barns
  • • Clean, dry bedding
  • • Sufficient space for movement

Bird Health Tips

Diet & Nutrition

  • • Species-appropriate pellet diet
  • • Fresh fruits and vegetables daily
  • • Avoid avocado, chocolate, caffeine
  • • Fresh water changed daily

Cage & Environment

  • • Spacious cage for wing spreading
  • • Natural perches of varying sizes
  • • Safe toys for mental stimulation
  • • Proper lighting (10-12 hours)

Health Signs

  • • Monitor droppings daily
  • • Watch for changes in behavior
  • • Check for respiratory issues
  • • Annual avian vet checkups

Social Needs

  • • Daily interaction and socialization
  • • Out-of-cage time in safe area
  • • Mental stimulation with training
  • • Consider companion birds
